### Step-by-Step Demo Mastery

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Play the Demo First">
    The demo covers early story content and introduces Parry, Deflect, Issen, Oni Armament, and soul absorption. Use it as a combat tutorial rather than rushing to the boss.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Move Before You Attack">
    Musashi can dodge enemy pressure. Repeated dodging fills the Reflex Dodge gauge for a Reflex Combo. Circle enemies, dodge attacks, and look for safe openings.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Prioritize Parry and Deflect">
    Blade-on-blade combat centers on parrying and deflecting. Focus on reacting to enemy swings and countering when a clash creates space. The demo teaches this rhythm.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Look for Issen Timing">
    Issen is a high-impact critical attack. Do not spam it. Wait for a clear enemy commitment and use the opening created by a strong defensive read. Practice on smaller enemies first.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Absorb Souls Safely">
    The Oni Gauntlet absorbs Genma souls for power. Absorb souls after an enemy is down, a clean counter, or when there's enough distance from the next attack.
  </Step>
  <Step num="6" title="Save Oni Armament for Pressure">
    Oni Armament provides a powerful burst. Use it when a tougher enemy is pressuring you, a boss opening appears, or when you need significant damage.
  </Step>
  <Step num="7" title="Treat Sasaki Ganryu as Your First Exam">
    Sasaki Ganryu is the formidable boss of the Kiyomizu-dera demo. Focus on reading his attacks, surviving the first phase, and using your techniques only when timing is clear. The goal is to understand the full combat loop.
  </Step>
</Steps>
