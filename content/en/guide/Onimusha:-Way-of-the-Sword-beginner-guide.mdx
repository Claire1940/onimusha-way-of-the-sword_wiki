<Section icon={Target} title="Mastering Defensive Techniques">
Effective defense is just as crucial as offense in Onimusha: Way of the Sword. The game offers two primary defensive maneuvers: parrying and deflecting. While both involve precise timing, their effects on the enemy and your combat flow differ significantly.

### Parry vs. Deflect

| Feature         | Parry (Samurai Way)                                    | Deflect (Shizu Way)                                   |
| :-------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- | :---------------------------------------------------- |
| **Input**       | Hit Guard during enemy attack animation                | Hold Guard, press Attack just before hit              |
| **Effect**      | Instantly shuts down enemy attack, safe opening        | Leaves 50/50 situation, enemy may follow up           |
| **Benefit**     | Builds **Blaze Gauge**, creates attack windows          | Obliterates massive chunk of enemy stamina            |
| **Outcome**     | Allows for immediate punishment                        | Breaks enemy posture for instant break opportunities  |
| **Risk**        | Low, creates a safe opening                            | Medium, requires quick follow-up or readiness for next attack |

<Callout type="warning" title="Timing is Everything">
Both parrying and deflecting require precise timing. Practice these moves extensively, especially against different enemy types, to internalize the attack patterns and maximize their effectiveness.
</Callout>

</Section>

| Mechanic          | Function                                            | Activation / Build-up                                  | Strategic Use                                      |
| :---------------- | :-------------------------------------------------- | :----------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------- |
| **Omni Gauntlet** | Captures souls of defeated Genma                    | Defeating Genma enemies                                | Powers up special attacks                            |
