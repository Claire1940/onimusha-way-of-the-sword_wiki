  <Step num="1" title="Enable Game Mode">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Shader Compilation">
    Allow the game to complete shader compilation at the main menu. This prevents "shader struggle" stutters during the transition into cinematic boss duels.

  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="SSD Optimization">
