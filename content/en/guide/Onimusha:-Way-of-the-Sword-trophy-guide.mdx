<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Practice Perfect Guarding">

  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Elemental Magic">
    Each weapon is imbued with an elemental magic (Thunder, Fire, Wind). Don't hoard your magic; use it strategically to clear groups of enemies, stagger bosses, or deal massive damage. Upgrading your magic will increase its potency and duration.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Chain Soul Absorptions">

  </Step>
