<Section icon={Swords} title="The Legend of Musashi and the Oni Gauntlet">

In this reimagining of the franchise, players step into the sandals of a young, fierce **Miyamoto Musashi**. Capcom has made a landmark decision by using the likeness of **Toshiro Mifune**, the iconic star of *Seven Samurai* and *Yojimbo*, to portray the protagonist. This choice anchors the game in the "Chambara" cinema tradition, providing a gritty, authentic feel to the blood-soaked duels.

Musashi's primary tool of destruction is the **Oni Gauntlet**, a mystical artifact that grants him the power to stand against the Genma. This gauntlet is not merely a weapon but a progression system that dictates how Musashi evolves throughout the story.

**Core Gauntlet Functions:**
- **Soul Absorption**: Slayed Genma release orbs (Red, Blue, Yellow) that are sucked into the gauntlet.
- **Elemental Imbuement**: The gauntlet can channel fire, lightning, and wind into Musashi's katanas.
- **The Voice**: A mysterious entity resides within the artifact, guiding Musashi through the twisted streets of Kyoto.

<Callout type="info" title="Soul Types and Effects">
- **Red Souls**: Used as currency to upgrade weapons and the Oni Gauntlet.
- **Blue Souls**: Restores Musashi's Magic/Elemental meter.
- **Yellow Souls**: Restores Musashi's Vitality (Health).
</Callout>

<Section icon={Shield} title="Combat Mastery and Enemy Types">

The Genma are monstrosities from the underworld, brought into the living world by the Malice enveloping the capital. Combat in **Onimusha: Way of the Sword ps5** is tactical; button-mashing will often lead to a quick death.

**Key Combat Mechanics:**
- **Issen (Critical Hit)**: Timing an attack precisely as an enemy strikes results in an instant kill for most Genma.
- **Deflection**: Blocking at the last second staggers foes, opening them up for a flurry of strikes.
- **Oni Awakening**: Once the gauntlet is fully charged with souls, Musashi can enter a brief state of invulnerability and massive damage output.
