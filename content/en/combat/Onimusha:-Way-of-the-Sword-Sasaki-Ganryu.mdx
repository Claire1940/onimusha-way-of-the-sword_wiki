<Section icon={Target} title="Ganryu's Combat Style and Oni Gauntlet Abilities">
Sasaki Ganryu's fighting style is a blend of traditional swordsmanship and the dark power of his Oni Gauntlet. He is known for his signature "Swallow Cut" (Tsubame Gaeshi), a swift, unpredictable technique that can catch even the most seasoned warriors off guard. The Oni Gauntlet not only enhances his physical strength and speed but also allows him to unleash devastating energy attacks. Players must learn to anticipate his movements and exploit openings to stand a chance against him.

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Do not underestimate the power of Ganryu's Oni Gauntlet. His enhanced attacks can inflict significant damage and may have elemental properties or status effects. Always prioritize dodging and creating distance when he charges up his gauntlet.
</Callout>
