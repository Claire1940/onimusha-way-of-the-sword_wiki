<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat Mechanics and Gameplay Expectations">
*Onimusha: Way of the Sword* promises to deliver the fast-paced, challenging combat that fans of the series expect, while introducing new elements with Miyamoto Musashi as the protagonist. The gameplay trailers emphasize precise timing and strategic use of Musashi's abilities.

The combat system centers around a blend of offensive and defensive maneuvers. Parrying and deflecting enemy attacks open windows for powerful counter-attacks, most notably the Issen. This critical hit mechanic rewards skilled players with significant damage output and stylish takedowns. Soul absorption, a staple of the *Onimusha* series, allows Musashi to gather souls from defeated enemies to restore health, magic, or upgrade his weapons and armor. The Oni Armament adds another layer of combat depth, providing unique skills and abilities that can turn the tide of battle.

<Callout type="warning" title="Mastering Issen">
The **Issen** counter-attack is a high-risk, high-reward move. While incredibly powerful, mistiming it can leave Musashi vulnerable to enemy attacks. Practice in the demo to perfect your timing!
</Callout>
