<Section icon={Shield} title="Strategies for Battling Enemies">
Successfully navigating the world of Onimusha: Way of the Sword requires a deep understanding of its combat mechanics. Against the diverse range of **enemies**, Musashi's Oni Gauntlet and swordplay techniques are his greatest assets.

<Steps>
  <Step num="1" title="Master Parry and Deflect">
    These are your primary defensive tools. Parry directly counters an enemy's attack, creating an opening, while deflect helps interrupt aggressive patterns and reset the fight. Practice these timings against weaker Genma to build muscle memory.
  </Step>
  <Step num="2" title="Utilize Issen">
    The signature critical attack of the Onimusha series, Issen rewards precise timing. It's not meant to be spammed; instead, wait for a clear enemy commitment and use the opening created by a successful defensive move. This is especially potent against tougher **enemies**.
  </Step>
  <Step num="3" title="Absorb Souls Strategically">
    The Oni Gauntlet allows Musashi to absorb Genma souls, which convert into power. Always absorb souls when it's safe – after an enemy is downed, following a clean counter, or when there's sufficient distance from the next incoming attack. This powers up Musashi's abilities.
  </Step>
  <Step num="4" title="Deploy Oni Armament">
    Oni Armament attacks provide a supernatural burst of force. Save these for high-pressure moments, such as when facing a stronger **enemy**, exploiting a boss's vulnerability, or when normal sword pressure isn't enough to break through defenses.
  </Step>
  <Step num="5" title="Leverage Oni Strength and Agility">
    Oni Strength is perfect for breaking through enemy guards and environmental obstacles. Oni Agility grants explosive movement, useful for quick repositioning in combat or traversing specific environmental challenges. These powers are vital for both combat and exploration.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout type="success" title="Effective Combat Flow">
A typical successful combat flow involves reading enemy attacks, using parry/deflect to create an opening, landing an Issen or a quick combo, absorbing souls, and then repositioning for the next engagement.
</Callout>

</Section>
