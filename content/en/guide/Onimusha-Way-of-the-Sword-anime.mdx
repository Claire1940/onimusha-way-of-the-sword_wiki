<Section icon={Target} title="Core Gameplay Mechanics">

Combat in Way of the Sword emphasizes technical precision. Unlike previous entries, players manage stamina to execute powerful counter-attacks. Below is a breakdown of the primary combat systems you will utilize.

| Mechanic | Description | Effect |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **One-Handed Slash** | Quick, low-stamina attack | Best for chip damage and crowd control |
| **Two-Handed Slash** | Stronger, long windup | Ideal for breaking enemy posture |
| **Deflect/Parry** | Well-timed block | Reflects attacks and depletes foe stamina |
| **Break Issen** | Finisher move | Instantly kills standard Genma |

<Callout type="tip">
Always keep an eye on your magic gauge. You must absorb blue orbs to unlock the power of your magical weapons, as they are not available for standard use.
</Callout>
