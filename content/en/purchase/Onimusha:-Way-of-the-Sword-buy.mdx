Onimusha: Way of the Sword brings a fresh, more challenging approach to the series, often compared to Dark Souls in its difficulty. Players take on the role of Miyamoto Musashi, a half-demon, half-man Onimusha, in an alternate Edo-period Kyoto. The combat system emphasizes aggression, parrying, and strategic dodging, rewarding players who master its intricate mechanics.
