<Section icon={Zap} title="The Oni Gauntlet vs The Shinobi Prosthetic">

A major differentiator in the **Onimusha Way of the Sword vs Sekiro** comparison is the utility of the protagonist's left arm. Wolf's Shinobi Prosthetic is a Swiss-army knife of tools (shurikens, firecrackers, axes). Miyamoto Musashi's **Oni Gauntlet** is a mystical artifact that functions as a source of raw power and character progression.

The Oni Gauntlet serves four primary functions:
1.  **Soul Absorption**: Drawing in Genma souls to power up weapons and abilities.
2.  **Oni Strength**: Breaking through heavy shields and environmental obstacles.
3.  **Oni Agility**: Enabling explosive dashes and supernatural traversal.
4.  **Oni Armament**: Transmuting Musashi's katana into a weapon of pure energy for a limited time.

| Gauntlet Power | Combat Application | Exploration Use |
|:---|:---|:---|
| **Oni Strength** | Shatters enemy guard / Heavy damage | Breaks sealed doors / Crumbles walls |
| **Oni Agility** | Rapid repositioning / Evasive dashes | Wall running / Long-distance jumps |
| **Oni Armament** | Massive burst damage / Elemental procs | Lighting torches / Powering shrines |
| **Soul Absorption** | Regains mana and health | Unlocks progression gates |

<Callout type="warning" title="Critical Warning">
Soul absorption is not just a post-battle reward; it is a combat mechanic. You must find safe openings to absorb souls, as being hit during the animation will interrupt the process and leave Musashi vulnerable.
</Callout>
