### Primary Sword Mechanics
Musashi's primary sword is his most reliable tool. It offers two distinct attack styles:
- **One-handed Attack (Square button)**: Swift, versatile, and excellent for chaining combos and maintaining pressure.
- **Two-handed Attack (Triangle button)**: Slower, more powerful, and potentially useful against shielded enemies or for breaking guard.

While the demo primarily showcased the effectiveness of one-handed attacks, the two-handed option is expected to play a more crucial role against tougher, later-game adversaries.

<Section icon={Shield} title="The Power of Souls and Oni Armaments">
Central to Musashi's combat prowess is his sentient gauntlet, which absorbs the souls of defeated enemies. These souls are not just a currency but a vital resource that directly impacts your combat effectiveness and access to powerful special **Onimusha: Way of the Sword weapons**.

### Soul Types and Their Uses

| Soul Type | Primary Function | Secondary Effect | Usage |
| :-------- | :--------------- | :--------------- | :---- |
| **Yellow Souls** | Replenish health | Boost stamina recovery | Essential for survival, especially during intense battles. |
| **Red Souls** | In-game currency | Upgrade weapons/armor | Used to acquire upgrades and new items. |
| **Blue Souls** | Power Oni Armaments | Grant special attacks | Activates devastating special weapon abilities. |

<Callout type="warning" title="Strategic Soul Management">
While yellow souls replenish health, relying solely on them can be risky. Prioritize absorbing souls based on your immediate needs – health for survival, blue souls for offensive bursts.
</Callout>

### Oni Armaments
Oni Armaments are special weapons or abilities powered by blue souls. These deliver significant damage and are crucial for overcoming formidable demons and boss encounters. Using Oni Armaments effectively also generates yellow souls, creating a dynamic cycle of offense and recovery.

| Oni Armament Type | Effect | Activation | Benefits |
| :---------------- | :----- | :--------- | :------- |
| **Special Attacks** | High burst damage | Consumes Blue Souls | Excellent for crowd control or focusing on single targets. |
| **Empowered Strikes** | Enhanced basic attacks | Active while Blue Soul gauge is full | Increased damage and stagger potential. |

### Defensive and Offensive Maneuvers

| Technique | Description | Effect | Timing |
| :-------- | :---------- | :----- | :----- |
| **Parry** | Block an incoming attack with precise timing. | Deflects damage, creates openings, can "steer" enemies. | Just before enemy strike connects. |
| **Perfect Dodge** | Evade an attack at the last moment. | Grants temporary invincibility, provides combat buffs. | Just as attack is about to land. |

| **Projectile Deflection** | Bat away or reflect incoming arrows. | Negates ranged threats, can damage enemies. | Timing a sword swipe against incoming projectiles. |
