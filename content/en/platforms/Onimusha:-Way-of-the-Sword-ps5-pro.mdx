<Section icon={Swords} title="Gameplay and Combat Mechanics">

Onimusha: Way of the Sword plunges players into the shoes of Miyamoto Musashi, a fierce samurai wielding the mystical Oni Gauntlet. This artifact grants him power beyond human limits, essential for battling the demonic Genma. The combat is characterized by intense swordplay and gritty, blood-soaked brawls.

<Callout type="warning" title="Mastering the Oni Gauntlet">
The Oni Gauntlet isn't just for brute force; it allows Musashi to absorb Genma souls, converting them into a power source. Mastering this ability is crucial for enhancing your character and unlocking new capabilities.
</Callout>
