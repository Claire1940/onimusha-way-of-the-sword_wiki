Onimusha: Way of the Sword plunges players into a dark fantasy rendition of early Edo-period Kyoto, where the line between reality and nightmare blurs. The game's narrative follows the legendary samurai, Miyamoto Musashi, as he seeks to prove his unmatched skill with the blade. However, his path takes an unexpected turn when he is thrust into a battle against the demonic Genma, creatures from the underworld that have infested Kyoto due to a malevolent force known as Malice.

The Genma are the primary antagonists in the **Onimusha Way of the Sword story**, monstrous beings from the underworld whose presence has plunged Kyoto into chaos. These creatures are brought forth by the Malice twisting the world, and Musashi's quest is inextricably linked to eradicating them.

Beyond the common Genma, players will face "Formidable Foes" – powerful opponents whose martial prowess can rival Musashi's own. These duels are designed to test the player's mastery of the game's intricate combat system, demanding precise parries, deflects, and Issen strikes. Shuten Doji, the colossal Genma of Mount Oe, is one such formidable foe, promising an epic encounter.
