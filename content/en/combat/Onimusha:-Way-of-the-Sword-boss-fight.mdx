<Section icon={Trophy} title="Strategic Elements in Boss Fights">
Beyond parrying, **Onimusha: Way of the Sword boss fight** introduces several strategic layers to combat. Successful parries build up an Oni gauge, which, when full, allows Musashi to unleash powerful combos with magical Oni weapons. For example, in the demo, daggers offered a flurry of strikes.

Depleting Sasaki's stamina bar provides an opportunity for a powerful strike. Players can choose to target specific body parts for different orb rewards:

- **Lower Body**: Provides blue and yellow orbs, essential for restoring health and Oni weapon charges.

This system adds a tactical depth, forcing players to consider not just how to win, but how to optimize their rewards from each encounter.
