Combat in *Onimusha: Way of the Sword* is a visceral experience that rewards precision and timing. Musashi wields a single katana, allowing for quick one-handed attacks or powerful two-handed strikes. Understanding the core mechanics is crucial for survival, especially when facing hordes of Genma.

Magic weapons are also available, but unlike previous *Onimusha* titles, they require filling a magic gauge by absorbing blue orbs. Unleashing their power will deplete the gauge, making them temporarily unavailable.
