<Section icon={Shield} title="Gameplay Features and Story Overview">
*Onimusha: Way of the Sword* promises a rich gameplay experience rooted in the series' core mechanics while introducing exciting new features. Players will step into the shoes of Miyamoto Musashi, a legendary swordsman in a turbulent Edo-period Kyoto, battling against the monstrous Genma.

The game's combat system is a major highlight, emphasizing **real-time physics-based sword clashes**. When Musashi and an enemy attack simultaneously, their blades will connect dynamically, creating fluid and impactful encounters. This system is designed to add a new layer of immersion and tension to every duel.

**Key Gameplay Mechanics:**
- **Oni Gauntlet**: Wield a mystical artifact that grants Musashi supernatural strength, allowing him to slay Genma and absorb their souls.
- **Environmental Interaction**: Utilize the environment to your advantage, kicking enemies into hazards or blinding them.
- **Weak Point Targeting**: Special moves enable targeting specific enemy weak points for massive damage or extra experience.
- **Soul Collection**: The classic mechanic returns, allowing Musashi to absorb the essence of fallen foes to power up his abilities and the Oni Gauntlet.

| Feature             | Description                                          | Impact on Gameplay                                |
| :------------------ | :--------------------------------------------------- | :------------------------------------------------ |
| **Physics-Based Combat** | Dynamic blade clashes, no canned animations         | Enhanced immersion, realistic duels               |
| **Oni Gauntlet**    | Grants supernatural powers, absorbs Genma souls      | Core progression, ability enhancement             |
| **Environmental Advantage** | Interact with surroundings for strategic combat    | Adds tactical depth, varied encounters            |
| **Soul Absorption** | Collect souls from defeated Genma                    | Powers up abilities, unlocks new skills          |
