<Section icon={Swords} title="Combat and the Oni Gauntlet Explained">
Combat in **Onimusha: Way of the Sword** is a refined blend of precise swordplay and supernatural Oni powers. Miyamoto Musashi's primary tool, the Oni Gauntlet, is central to both offense and defense, allowing players to absorb Genma souls to enhance abilities. Mastering timing for parries, deflects, and the signature Issen attack will be crucial for survival.

<Callout type="warning" title="Issen Timing is Key">
Do not spam Issen. Wait for a clear enemy commitment and use it only after a strong defensive read for maximum impact.
</Callout>

| Combat Mechanic | Summary | Use Case |
|---|---|---|
| **Parry** | A core defensive action for blade-on-blade combat. | Use when an enemy commits to a clear melee strike to create counterattack opportunities. |
| **Deflect** | A timing-based defensive tool supporting tactical sword clashes. | Interrupt aggressive enemy patterns and reset the fight in your favor. |
| **Issen** | The signature Onimusha critical attack, rewarding precise timing. | Execute after learning an enemy's attack rhythm for high damage payoff. |
| **Soul Absorption** | The Oni Gauntlet absorbs Genma souls to convert into power. | Absorb souls after an enemy is down, a clean counter, or when safe from immediate attack. |
| **Oni Armament** | Supernatural attack options for heavier burst damage. | Use against stronger enemies, during boss openings, or in high-pressure situations. |
| **Oni Strength** | Oni power to break through stalemates and enemy defenses. | Employ when an enemy is heavily guarding or when the route requires breaking obstacles. |
| **Oni Agility** | Oni power focused on explosive movement and traversal. | Utilize for fast repositioning, reaching new areas, or traversing walls. |
| **Reflex Combo** | A powerful combo unleashed after filling the Reflex Dodge gauge. | Activates after continuously dodging enemy attacks, rewarding survival and pattern recognition. |

</Section>
