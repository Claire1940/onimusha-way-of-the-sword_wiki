Onimusha: Way of the Sword thrusts players into a dark Edo-period Kyoto, demanding mastery of its intricate swordplay. The combat system revolves around precise timing, strategic defense, and powerful counterattacks, making every encounter with the Genma a test of skill.

<Section icon={Sparkles} title="The Power of the Oni Gauntlet">
Miyamoto Musashi's journey is intrinsically linked to the mysterious Oni Gauntlet, a powerful artifact that grants him supernatural abilities. This gauntlet is not merely a story element; it's a central pillar of both combat and exploration, providing Musashi with unique offensive and traversal options.

<Callout type="warning" title="Strategic Oni Use">
While powerful, Oni Armament and other Oni abilities should be used strategically. Conserving your Oni energy for tougher encounters or critical exploration moments can turn the tide of battle.
</Callout>

The Oni Gauntlet enables powerful **Oni Armament** attacks, which provide a burst of force when normal swordplay isn't enough. Beyond direct combat, the gauntlet also bestows **Oni Strength** for breaking through obstacles and enemy defenses, and **Oni Agility** for explosive movement and accessing new areas.
