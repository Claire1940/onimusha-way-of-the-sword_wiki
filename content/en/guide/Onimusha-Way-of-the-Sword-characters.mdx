<Section icon={Target} title="Character Progression and Mechanics">

Character growth in *Onimusha: Way of the Sword* is intrinsically linked to the combat loop. As you defeat Genma, you absorb different types of souls that replenish your vital resources. This mechanic ensures that even when you are low on health or special energy, the game provides the necessary tools to continue the fight if you remain aggressive.

| Resource Type | Effect | Acquisition Method |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Health Souls** | Restores HP | Defeating enemies when low HP |
| **Special Energy** | Powers Ultimate | Hitting enemies / Active combat |
| **Stamina Gauge** | Enables Actions | Passive regen / Combat rhythm |
