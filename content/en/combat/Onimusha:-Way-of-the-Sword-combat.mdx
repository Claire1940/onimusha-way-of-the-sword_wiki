<Section icon={Target} title="The Power of Issen and Break Issen">
The Issen mechanic is arguably the most iconic and satisfying aspect of Onimusha's combat, and it returns with renewed importance in Way of the Sword. Issen allows for instant enemy execution when timed perfectly, dismembering foes and granting significant combat advantages.

Break Issen takes this a step further. By depleting an opponent's stamina through strikes and parries, players can unleash this powerful move for an instant kill. While bosses cannot be instantly executed with Break Issen, it still deals massive damage and offers a choice between greater damage output or more soul collection. Consecutive parries or dodges further enhance Musashi's combat prowess, enabling rapid successive executions or multi-hit attacks.

<Section icon={Shield} title="Utilizing Oni Armaments and Soul Collection">
Musashi is equipped with a sentient gauntlet, a vital tool for both offense and resource management. This gauntlet consumes the souls of defeated enemies, providing three distinct types of souls, each with a crucial role in gameplay:

| Soul Type | Primary Function               | Secondary Benefit (Oni Armaments) |
| :-------- | :----------------------------- | :-------------------------------- |
| **Yellow** | Replenishes Player Health      | Generated by hitting enemies with Oni Armaments |
| **Red**    | Used for Upgrades and Purchases | N/A                               |
| **Blue**   | Powers Oni Armaments           | Enables use of special, high-damage weapons    |

Oni Armaments are special weapons activated by Blue Souls, capable of dealing significant damage to enemies. Hitting foes with these armaments, in turn, generates Yellow Souls, creating a dynamic loop between offensive and defensive resource management. The gauntlet also grants Musashi "Oni Vision," allowing him to detect all demons and enemies within an area, providing a tactical advantage in exploration and combat preparation.

<Callout type="success" title="Efficient Soul Management">
Prioritize collecting Red Souls for critical upgrades early on. During combat, strategically use Blue Souls for Oni Armaments to both deal damage and replenish your health via Yellow Souls.
</Callout>

</Section>
